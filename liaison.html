Parent Liaison Team

Your official connection between the Air Force Academy and the USAFA parent community — supporting families through every milestone from I-Day to graduation.

Mission

The Parent Liaison Team directly serves the USAFA parent community as project officers for all institutional events requiring parent involvement, provides oversight and information to all USAFA parents' clubs, and ensures strong communication lines between USAFA and the parent community.
