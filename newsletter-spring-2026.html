Alabama Cadet Spotlight

Celebrating our cadets from the Heart of Dixie

★ Featured Cadet ★
Class of 2028

Hunter Johnston

📍 Madison, Alabama

Before answering the call to serve at the United States Air Force Academy, Hunter Johnston demonstrated exceptional leadership as the Wing Commander of his local JROTC program at Bob Jones High School in Madison, Alabama. A natural competitor, he also served as captain of the tennis team, learning valuable lessons about strategy, discipline, and teamwork on the court.

Hunter's journey to USAFA included a year at Marion Military Institute, where he further honed his military bearing and academic foundation. Now thriving at the Academy, Hunter is pursuing a degree in Aerospace Engineering—a challenging major that reflects his passion for flight and space. His ultimate goal? To earn his wings and become a pilot in the United States Air Force.

Alabama USAFA Founders Day Dinner

📍 Location TBA • 🗓️ April 2026

Join the USAFA Parents Club of Alabama as we celebrate Founders Day—a tradition commemorated on April 1st to honor the historic establishment of the United States Air Force Academy by President Dwight D. Eisenhower in 1954. This special dinner brings together parents, cadets, graduates, and supporters from across Alabama to celebrate the Academy's legacy and the remarkable achievements of our future Air Force and Space Force leaders.

Our Alabama chapter event will feature guest speakers, cadet recognition, fellowship, and an opportunity to strengthen the bonds within our USAFA family. Don't miss this chance to connect with fellow parents and celebrate our cadets!

Preparing for Basic Cadet Training

Essential tips for the Class of 2030

What to Expect & How to Prepare

Basic Cadet Training (BCT) is a six-week transformation program. Here's how to arrive ready.

💪 Physical Preparation
  • Establish a daily 30-minute aerobic workout routine, including running at least 2 miles
  • Practice pull-ups, push-ups, sit-ups, and standing long jump—these are tested during BCT
  • Build stamina for the 1.5-mile aerobics test and prepare for altitude adjustment in Colorado
  • Break in comfortable boots and water-resistant sandals to prevent blisters during Jacks Valley
📚 Knowledge Preparation
  • Study the Cadet Honor Code and understand its principles and application
  • Learn Air Force and Space Force heritage, military customs, and courtesies
  • Review the Chain of Command and understand military rank structure
  • Familiarize yourself with reporting procedures and drill basics
🧠 Mental Preparation
  • Develop stress management techniques—BCT is designed to push your limits
  • Practice focusing on tasks while under pressure; build mental resilience
  • Understand that your Cadre (upper-class instructors) are there to help you succeed
  • Prepare for teamwork—BCT emphasizes cohesion and supporting your fellow cadets
📋 Key Dates for Class of 2030
  • June 25, 2026: In-Processing Day—BCT Begins
  • June 26, 2026: Swearing-In Ceremony (4:00 PM, Stillman Parade Field)
  • July 18, 2026: March Out to Jacks Valley (5.5-mile march to field training)
  • July 31, 2026: BCT Culminating Event & Acceptance into Cadet Wing
