Who We Are

About Our Club

The USAFA Parents Club of Alabama is a volunteer-run, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Alabama families whose sons and daughters have earned the honor of attending the United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

We serve as a bridge between families at home and their cadets at the Academy, working in coordination with Academy officials and liaison officers — providing guidance, community, and resources from the moment of appointment through commissioning day.

  • Care package drives and morale support for cadets during BCT
  • Informational meetings for newly-appointed families
  • Parent-to-parent mentorship and peer support network
  • Recognition of Alabama cadet achievements and milestones
  • Coordination with regional USAFA Liaison Officers
  • Connection to the National USAFA Parents Club network
