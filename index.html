Who We Are

About Our Club

The USAFA Parents Club of Alabama is a volunteer-run, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Alabama families whose sons and daughters have earned the honor of attending the United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

We serve as a bridge for families at home and their cadets at the Academy, working in coordination with Academy officials and liaison officers — providing guidance, community, and resources from the moment of appointment through commissioning day.

Club Leadership

Your club officers are fellow USAFA parents who have walked the same path and are honored to serve Alabama's cadet families.

Kari Johnston — President

Kari is the proud mother of three, including Cadet Hunter Johnston, Class of 2028, and lives in Madison, Alabama. She has been a nurse for 23 years and currently serves as a NICU nurse at Huntsville Hospital, bringing the same dedication and compassion to cadet families that she provides to her patients.

Outside of work, Kari enjoys hiking local trails and giving back through her church and community. She is honored to serve as President of the USAFA Parents Club of Alabama.
