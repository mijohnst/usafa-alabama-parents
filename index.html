Who We Are
About Our Club

The USAFA Parents Club of Alabama is a volunteer-run, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Alabama families whose sons and daughters have earned the honor of attending the United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

We serve as a bridge between families at home and their cadets at the Academy ‚Äî providing guidance, community, and resources from the moment of appointment through commissioning day.

  • Annual scholarship awards for USAFA-bound Alabama students
  • Care package drives and morale support for cadets during BCT
  • Informational meetings for newly-appointed families
  • Parent-to-parent mentorship and peer support network
  • Coordination with regional USAFA Liaison Officers
  • Connection to the National USAFA Parents Club network

Scholarship Program

Each year, the USAFA Parents Club of Alabama proudly awards scholarships to qualified Alabama students who have received official appointments to the Air Force Academy.

2025 Scholarship Applications Open

Alabama high school seniors who have received an appointment to USAFA are encouraged to apply. Awards are merit-based and consider academic achievement, extracurricular involvement, and community service. Applications are reviewed by the club's scholarship committee each spring.
