A Message from Our President

Welcome to the USAFA Parents Club of Alabama

Dear USAFA Alabama Families,

I am absolutely thrilled to connect with you today! As we look toward the future of our club, I am filled with pride for the young men and women we represent—our incredible cadets who are working so hard at the Academy. While they are far from home, our mission is to ensure they always feel the support of their Alabama roots, and we have some exciting new ways to make that happen!

First, I want to give a massive shout-out to our dedicated team of volunteer officers. Vice President Susan Jackson, Secretary Shelley Kavlick, and Treasurer Mike Sheehy have been working tirelessly behind the scenes for months, meeting as often as once a week to move our club forward. I also want to extend a special thank you to our web designer, Mike Johnston, for his incredible vision and hard work.

The Wait is Over: Revealing Our New Website!

We are so excited to officially reveal our brand-new website! This was designed specifically to help us connect with you and for you to connect with us. It is your one-stop shop for upcoming events, the latest news from USAFA, and so much more. Our big plans to support our amazing cadets start right here!

Investing in Our Cadets

To continue our mission of supporting our future officers, we need your help. We are asking all families to get current on their parent dues. These funds go directly toward the programs and initiatives that celebrate our cadets' milestones.

  • Annual Dues: $75 per year
  • Four-Year Discount: $275 (covers your cadet's entire journey!)

Our new website makes this easier than ever with multiple payment options available.

Save the Date: Annual Meeting via Zoom

We want you to be a part of your cadet's journey! Whether you have a little time to give or a lot, there are many ways to get involved. Please join us for our Annual USAFA Alabama Parents Club Meeting:

  • When: Sunday, April 12th, 2026
  • Time: 4:00 PM Central
  • Where: Via Zoom (link will be sent to members)

During this meeting, we will introduce the board, share our by-laws, discuss upcoming fundraising, and reveal our exciting plans for our outstanding cadets.

It is truly my pleasure to serve alongside such a dedicated group of parents. Together, we are supporting the future leaders of this great nation.

God bless each of your families.

Sincerely,

Kari Johnston

President, USAFA Alabama Parents Club
