Our Valued Sponsors

Supporting the families and cadets of the Alabama USAFA Parents Club through generous partnerships and community engagement.

Premier Sponsor

Yulista Holding, LLC

Huntsville, Alabama

Yulista is an Alaska Native Corporation and trusted aerospace and defense partner supporting military and civilian customers with aviation maintenance, engineering, logistics, and mission support services worldwide. Based in Huntsville, Alabama, their world-class facilities and dedicated team of over 800 professionals serve hundreds of contracts across aviation, aerospace, maritime, ground systems, and cyber/IT domains.

As an Alaska Native Corporation, Yulista's mission extends beyond defense contracting—they are committed to serving people, elevating the lives of their teams and shareholders, while delivering value-added solutions and unparalleled customer experiences. Their heritage connects them to the Central Yup'ik and Athabascan communities, and their success helps sustain a way of life in the Calista Region of Alaska.

Supporting Our June Cadet Sendoff

$500

Yulista's generous contribution supports our annual June Cadet Sendoff event, helping families meet, enjoy lunch together, and provide care packages to cadets as they begin their journey at the United States Air Force Academy. This meaningful event strengthens the bonds between families and cadets during an important transition.

The Alabama USAFA Parents Club extends our deepest gratitude to Yulista for their outstanding support of our mission. Their commitment to service—both in defense of our nation and in support of our local community—exemplifies the values we hold dear. We are honored to have Yulista as our premier sponsor and proud to partner with an organization that shares our dedication to developing future Air Force leaders.
