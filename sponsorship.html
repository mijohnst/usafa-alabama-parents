About Yulista

Yulista is an Alaska Native Corporation and trusted aerospace and defense partner, headquartered right here in Huntsville, Alabama. With a dedicated team of over 800 professionals, Yulista delivers comprehensive aviation maintenance, engineering, logistics, and mission support services to military and civilian customers worldwide.

Their expertise spans aviation, aerospace, maritime, ground systems, and cyber/IT domains, providing fully integrated systems sustainment, technical support, manufacturing, training, engineering, program management, and professional services tailored to meet critical mission requirements.

Beyond defense contracting, Yulista's mission is deeply rooted in serving people. As an Alaska Native Corporation, their success helps sustain the way of life for Central Yup'ik and Athabascan communities in the Calista Region of Alaska, connecting their heritage to their commitment to elevating lives and delivering value-added solutions.

Supporting Our June Cadet Sendoff

Yulista's generous contribution directly supports our annual June Cadet Sendoff event, where families gather to meet, share a meal together, and provide care packages to cadets as they embark on their journey at the United States Air Force Academy. This meaningful event strengthens bonds during an important transition and demonstrates the power of community support.
