Before I-Day

Pre In-Processing (I-Day) Events

Appointee & Family Welcome Day — June 23, 2026

A special event at Doolittle Hall for incoming appointees and their families, held 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM. Registration is required.

  • Squadron meetups
  • Vendor booths
  • Family Membership sign-up
  • Information booth
  • Shopping at Doolittle Outfitters

I-Day

In-Processing Day (I-Day) — June 24, 2026

After you drop off your appointee, park at Falcon Stadium and board buses to be transported to Parent Program events.

Information Fair — Arnold Hall Ballroom

  • USAFA Helping Agencies
  • Representatives from Cadet Pay, Medical, and Sponsor Family Program

I-Day Picnic — Arnold Hall Patio (Sponsored by Colorado Parents' Club)

24 June 2026 • 7:30 AM – 2:00 PM • Arnold Hall, 2308 Cadet Drive, USAF Academy, CO 80840

  • Meet other USAFA families
  • Photo booth
  • Free breakfast and lunch provided
  • Write your first letter to your cadet — delivered directly to their BCT squadron
  • Visit campus support information tables

USAFA Planetarium Open House also available on I-Day.
