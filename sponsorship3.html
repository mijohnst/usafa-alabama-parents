Our Community Partners

We are deeply grateful to the businesses and individuals who support our mission to serve the families and cadets of the Alabama USAFA Parents Club. Their generosity makes our events, resources, and community possible.

Thank You to Our Sponsors

These outstanding partners share our commitment to supporting the next generation of Air Force leaders and strengthening our community.

Yulista Holding, LLC

Huntsville, Alabama

Premier Sponsor

June Cadet Sendoff 2025

Supporting families and cadets

$500

Yulista is an Alaska Native Corporation and trusted aerospace and defense partner, headquartered right here in Huntsville, Alabama. With a dedicated team of over 800 professionals, Yulista delivers comprehensive aviation maintenance, engineering, logistics, and mission support services to military and civilian customers worldwide across aviation, aerospace, maritime, ground systems, and cyber/IT domains.

Supporting Our Community

Yulista's generous contribution directly supports our annual June Cadet Sendoff event, where families gather to meet, share a meal together, and provide care packages to cadets as they embark on their journey at the United States Air Force Academy. This meaningful event strengthens bonds during an important transition and demonstrates the power of community support.

We extend our deepest gratitude to Yulista for their outstanding support of our mission. Their commitment to service—both in defense of our nation and in support of our local community—exemplifies the values we hold dear. We are honored to partner with an organization that shares our dedication to developing future Air Force leaders.
